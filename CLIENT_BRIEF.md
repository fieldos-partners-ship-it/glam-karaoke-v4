## Recommended Page Architecture

**Industry max for nightlife/entertainment venue:** 6–8 pages. Recommending **7 pages** — within gate.

```
/                    — Homepage
├── /rooms           — Rooms & Pricing (primary booking driver)
├── /menu            — Food & Drinks
├── /events          — Events & DJ Nights
├── /private-events  — Private Events / Party Booking (NEW — not in current nav)
├── /about           — About Glam
└── /reservations    — Book a Room (conversion page)
```

**Rationale per page:**

| Page | Rationale |
|------|-----------|
| `/` | Aspirational hero, room preview, vibe photography, social proof strip, happy hour callout, primary CTA to /reservations |
| `/rooms` | Three-tier room comparison (Duo / Group / Party Suite) with pricing, capacity, features, photos, booking CTA per room |
| `/menu` | Full food + drink menu; showcases Korean-American kitchen to elevate "bar" perception; drives longer stays |
| `/events` | Live DJ nights calendar; Friday @loxs1ck programming; themed nights; builds repeat visit traffic and Instagram content loop |
| `/private-events` | Dedicated landing for birthday parties, bachelorettes, corporate events; Party Suite focus; inquiry/booking form; highest revenue-per-booking page |
| `/about` | Founding story (opened 2022 to fill NoVA nightlife gap); press mentions (NorVA Mag, Arlington Mag); trust signals; Google review aggregate |
| `/reservations` | Primary conversion page — room selection, date/time picker or booking widget, phone CTA, confirmation flow |

**Note:** Gallery functionality should be embedded in homepage and /about rather than a standalone page. No standalone /gallery route recommended — galleries without conversion hooks are traffic dead-ends.
